Welcome to The Beard Blog

This is a place of learning and growth. Beard growth that is! Together we can learn how to care for our beloved facial hair.

Here is what you can learn.
Discover how best to clean your manly mane. Read our reviews of the top beard shampoos and conditioners.
When it comes to keeping your beard and skin soft and healthy there are many options for products. There are many oils, balms, butters, and creams to choose from. It may take a lot of trial and error to find the best solution for your face.
When styling there may be some overlap with the balms and creams, but generally a thicker wax product is used to hold the beard in place and provide a nice uniform appearance.
There are many beard trimming products to choose from. At minimum you should have a good pair of sheers, but you may also need electric clippers. Beard combs and brushes will also help with every part of propper grooming.

The Beard Blog is an amazing resource for anyone looking to learn everything there is to know about beards!
